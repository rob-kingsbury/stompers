Chapter I

Origins

Summer 2019
REC 06.15.2019

Rob Morrison

Vocals / Guitar

Interviewed: Nov 2024

"We didn't have a plan. We had a garage, some busted equipment, and this feeling that if we didn't do this, we'd regret it forever."

Four musicians. One shared obsession with the raw, unpolished sound of southern blues and rock.

They met through the usual channels — open mics, mutual friends, late nights arguing about Skynyrd vs. the Allman Brothers. By June 2019, they were rehearsing three times a week in Rob's garage.
