Our Story

Born in the Swamp

The Swamp City Stompers emerged from the murky waters of Eastern Ontario in 2019, bringing raw, unfiltered southern rock and swamp blues to stages across the region. What started as a few friends jamming in a garage has grown into a full-fledged blues revival.

The Sound

Our sound is a gritty blend of swamp blues, southern rock, and roots Americana. Think ZZ Top meets Creedence, filtered through the Canadian wilderness. Heavy guitar riffs, driving rhythms, and vocals that have been soaked in whiskey and woodsmoke.

The Mission

We believe in the power of live music to bring people together. Every show is a chance to shake off the week, stomp your feet, and feel something real. No backing tracks, no auto-tune, just raw energy and honest music.

"Music should make you move. If your feet aren't tapping, we're not doing our job."
