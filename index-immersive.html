The Band

Four friends, one bad idea, and a lot of good tone.

Swamp City Stompers smash together blues, classic rock, and a little outlaw country into a live show that never quite behaves itself.

From sticky-floor bar gigs to festival stages, the setlist leans into groove, grit, and big sing-along choruses. No tracks. No safety net. Just loud tubes, tight pockets, and the occasional guitar solo that goes on far too long.
